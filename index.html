Martin William Fingerhut

Martin Fingerhut pictured alongside his wife, Phyllis Fingerhut.

Martin Fingerhut, loving husband, father, and grandfather was born on September 17, 1934 in Williamsburg. He was the son of Jack and Gussie Fingerhut (née Teitelbaum). He earned his Bachelor's degree at Yeshiva University in New York in 1956 as well as a Bachelor of Laws at Brooklyn College in 1961. Martin further completed a postgraduate executive program at Stanford University in 1978. He worked as the senior vice president at Illinois Central Railroad in Chicago, beginning in 1969.

Martin was married to Phyllis Fingerhut (née Matthew) on August 19, 1958. Their children are Glenn, Laura, and Jill. Their grandchildren are grandfather of Ashley, Taylor, Kyle, Alexander, Jessica, Daniel, Kira, Jonah, Brianna, Gabriella, and Emma.
